Google Search Command

Sep 10th, 2012 7:57 pm

Perviously I have posted a blog about some tips and tricks of searching in google, later on I am wondering is that all? Obviously the answer is no, here is a full list of google search command. Use them with imagination and they will have the potential to become hundreds of google tricks.

cache:

Show the cached snapshot of a page Sample search: cache:www.google.com

The cache is the search engine’s copy of a page in its index. Google typically caches the first 101K of a page and not the images. The links to the caches are shown in Google’s search results and Page Info button on the Google toolbar can be used to access any cache available for the page.

Disable Internal Display in Lion

Apr 2nd, 2012 2:54 pm

Some MacBook Pro or Air users may want to disable their internal screen when the laptop is connected to an external display, this is generally achieved in two ways but since Mac OS X 10.7 the internal screen is more persistent and wants to stay on.

To disable the internal screen for OS X Lion based laptops, launch the Terminal and enter the following command:

sudo nvram boot-args="iog=0x0"
